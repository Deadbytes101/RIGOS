# Release Claims and Evidence Gates

RIGOS does not claim superiority over Hive OS, NiceHash, or another product before comparative physical evidence exists.

RIGOS fee claims apply to RIGOS only. They must never imply removal of the
bundled official XMRig binary's upstream donation behavior.

CPU-only superiority targets are measurable gates:

- agent idle CPU below 0.5%;
- agent RSS below 40 MiB;
- successful USB 2.0 boot on old AMD64;
- no illegal instruction on Athlon II/Phenom II;
- miner recovery below 15 seconds;
- thermal reaction below 3 seconds;
- immutable-root survival after power loss;
- last-known-good policy rollback;
- bounded USB write amplification;
- seven-day MoneroOcean and custom Stratum production runs;
- no cloud, billing, worker-limit, forced-pool, or RIGOS dev-fee dependency.

These remain targets until evidence packages prove them. v0.0.1 proves observation only and must not imply later lifecycle, thermal, USB-image, or production-duration behavior.
